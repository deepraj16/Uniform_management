‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI detection? ‚ñº
The system uses state-of-the-art computer vision models that achieve high accuracy in detecting uniform items. However, proper lighting and camera positioning are important for best results.
What happens if a student is not in complete uniform? ‚ñº
The system automatically flags the student as non-compliant and records which items are missing. Teachers can view all violations in the Violations page and take appropriate action.
Can I see a student's uniform history? ‚ñº
Yes! Click on any student in the Dashboard or Students page and select "View History" to see their complete uniform check record with photos.
How do I track attendance? ‚ñº
The Attendance page shows which students have checked in today (present) and which haven't (absent). Students are automatically marked present when they take their uniform photo.
What are the system requirements? ‚ñº
Students need a device with a working webcam and modern web browser. Teachers can access the dashboard from any device with internet connection.
